About Me

Hi there! My name is Liam White, and I am a 22-year-old Computer Science student studying at De Montfort University, Leicester. I've always been interested in technology and the way it can be used to solve complex problems, which is why I chose to study Computer Science. At De Montfort, I have been able to learn from knowledgeable and experienced professors who have been able to deepen my understanding of may different aspects of Computing. The coursework is challenging, but it is also very rewarding. I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, involving Object Oriented Development, Web Application Development, Database Design and Implementation, and much more. Overall, I feel that De Montfort University has provided me with an excellent education and the resources I need to achieve my goals. I am excited about the future and the opportunities that await me in the field of Computer Science.

Education

As a final year Computer Science student at De Montfort University (DMU), I have found the experience to be enriching and challenging in equal measure. The course is well-structured and provides a good balance between theory and practical application, with opportunities to work on real-world projects and gain hands-on experience.

The faculty members are knowledgeable, supportive, and approachable, and they are always available to provide guidance and feedback. The university has excellent facilities, including large computing laboratories and a well-stocked library, which provides access to the latest research and industry publications.

DMU has a vibrant and diverse student community, which provides ample opportunities to network and collaborate with peers from different backgrounds and cultures. The university has a strong focus on employability, with a dedicated career service that provides support and guidance on job hunting, interview techniques, and professional development.

See the timeline page for lists of what modules I took for each year, along with my grades for each.

Overall, I would recommend DMU to anyone looking to pursue a career in Computer Science or related fields. The university provides a supportive and stimulating environment, which allows students to develop their skills and achieve their full potential.

MMT Digital provides a range of services, including website design and development, digital strategy, user experience design, and content management. They work with a diverse range of clients, from small startups to large multinational corporations, across various industries such as finance, healthcare, and retail.

The agency is known for its customer-centric approach to digital design and development, with a focus on creating engaging, user-friendly experiences that drive business results. MMT Digital has won several awards for its work, including the 2020 Prolific North Tech Awards for Best Digital Agency.

Overall, MMT Digital is a trusted and respected digital agency with a strong reputation for delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of its clients.
